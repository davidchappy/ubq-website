About Uptown Brass

Based in Greenville, SC, the Uptown Brass Quintet was formed in early 2015 by a group of veteran brass performers and instructors. With a broad and tasteful repertoire that includes classical, light jazz, and elegant hymn settings, they are equally at home playing in a wedding ceremony as in a concert hall.
With a flexible schedule and accessible rates, you can add a touch of class to your wedding or event without the hassles and cost of dealing with an agency.
