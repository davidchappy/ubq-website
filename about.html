Meet the Players

In late 2014, what began as a casual phone conversation about some lost sheet music soon turned into the initial plans to form what would become Uptown Brass. Over the next few months, a few phone calls and conversations to local friends and brass players secured the addition members needed, and the group began rehearsaing in mid 2015.
For every player in Uptown Brass, our first goal is always to enjoy the thrill of creating beautiful music. Learn a little more about each member below.
